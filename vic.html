What you should know about My state

Akwa ibom as we all know "the land of promise" was born or created on the 23rd of september 1987 by the then Military Administration of General Ibrahim Badamosi Babangida.The creation of the state brought to fruition years of prolonged struggle by the people that occupied the mainland part of the former Cross River state.The present gorvernor of my state is His Excellency Mr Udom Gabriel Emmanuel
